About Me

I completed my Master of Technology in Water Engineering and Management from IIT Kharagpur, which strengthened my technical foundation in environmental systems, water infrastructure, and data-driven engineering workflows. I am a detail-oriented Data Analyst with a strong foundation in data exploration, visualization, and analytical problem‑solving. I specialize in working with datasets to identify patterns, clean and organize information, and translate findings into actionable insights for technical and business decision‑making.

What I Do

  • Conduct Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) to extract trends and insights
  • Build interactive dashboards and reports using Power BI and Tableau
  • Use Python (Pandas, NumPy) and SQL for data manipulation and querying
  • Analyze environmental and engineering datasets using GIS tools
  • Prepare structured technical documentation and data reports

Technical Skills

Career Goal

I aim to grow as a Data Analyst, contributing to data‑driven decisions in sectors such as environment, sustainability, water systems, public infrastructure, and operations analytics, while continuously enhancing my analytical and visualization skills.
